Case study
🔥
The problem
A D2C skincare brand was spending ₹3L/month across Meta, Google, and influencers with no unified view of what was actually working.

Monthly reports came from three separate tools, took the founder 6 hours to compile, and arrived 10 days after month-end — by which point the insights were already stale.

They suspected some campaigns were bleeding money but couldn't prove which ones.
⚙️
What I built
  • Pulled Meta Ads, Google Ads, and Shopify data into a single Power BI model with blended ROAS and CAC calculated across all channels
  • Dashboard refreshes daily and surfaces not just the numbers — but the decisions
  • Underperforming campaigns are automatically flagged when ROAS drops below 1.0, with estimated wasted spend called out at the top
  • The founder now opens this every Monday morning instead of waiting for a monthly PDF
📈
The outcome
  • Identified ₹47,200 in wasted spend across 4 campaigns running below break-even in the first 30 days
  • Paused them and reallocated to top 3 performers — Google brand search, Meta retargeting, and skincare-creator influencers
  • Blended ROAS moved from 2.1x → 3.4x
  • Monthly reporting time dropped from 6 hours → 10 minutes
  • CAC fell from ₹508 → ₹412
