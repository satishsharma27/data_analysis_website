There's your breakdown. The short version of why each one should be paused:

  • 1

    Anti-aging · Interest stack — wrong audience type. High-AOV skincare doesn't convert from cold interest targeting. These people need to have already shown intent before you spend money on them.

  • 2

    Acne range · Broad prospecting — classic ad fatigue. A frequency of 4.8 means the same person has seen the same creative almost 5 times. CTR has collapsed from 2.1% to 0.6% as a result.

  • 3

    Micro-influencer bundle — wrong niche. The influencers had decent reach but their followers weren't skincare buyers. Clicks don't equal purchases when the audience has no product intent.

  • 4

    YouTube pre-roll — wrong format for the goal. Pre-roll is a brand awareness play, not a sales driver. If you're measuring it by ROAS, it will always lose. Either change the format goal or move the budget to Google Shopping where the buyer intent already exists.
