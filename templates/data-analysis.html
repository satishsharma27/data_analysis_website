FAQ

Things you're probably wondering

How is this different from hiring in-house?+
An in-house analyst costs ₹12L+/year, takes 3 months to ramp up, and probably hasn't worked across 20+ brands. You get senior-level thinking without the headcount.
What if our data is a mess?+
Everyone's data is a mess. That's the job. The audit week is specifically for cleaning, connecting, and modelling whatever you have. You don't need to "get ready" before calling.
Do I need specific BI tools or a data warehouse?+
No. Shopify + Meta Ads + GA4 is enough. I'll recommend minimal tooling — usually Looker Studio for smaller brands, BigQuery + Looker for scale.
Who owns the dashboards and code?+
You do. All SQL, Python scripts, and dashboard files are handed over on day 8. If we part ways, you keep running without me.
Can you sign an NDA?+
Yes, always. Happy to sign before the first call if you prefer.
